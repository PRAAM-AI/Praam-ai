01

Document & data intelligence

Helping teams turn contracts, reports, forms, and operational spreadsheets into answers people can trust—grounded in your files, your policies, and your definitions of “done.”

  • Ingest and structure PDFs, scans, SOPs, and tabular extracts
  • Natural-language Q&A with citations back to source material
  • Summaries, comparisons, and light visualization for decision review
  • Architect for regulated or air-gapped environments when required
02

Cross-system workflow automation

Designing reliable handoffs across email, tickets, CRM, ERP, Slack, and bespoke internal tools—so requests don’t stall between systems or inboxes.

  • Classify and triage inbound channels with explicit escalation rules
  • Draft or send responses with human-in-the-loop where it matters
  • Create, enrich, or reconcile records in systems of record
  • Measure throughput, exceptions, and rework—not “automation theater”
03

AI-assisted engineering & delivery

Pairing engineers with agents that respect repository context, branching policy, and review culture—so drafts are proposals your team still owns.

  • Ground changes in repo layout, tests, and existing conventions
  • Trigger from tickets, specs, chat, or scheduled maintenance windows
  • Open pull requests or patches with rationale your reviewers expect
  • Slot into CI, approvals, and release governance you already use
