Products

Built for outcomes you can ship

Named offerings you can pilot, deploy, and extend—Ask Albi, workflow automation, and AgentX.

01

Ask Albi

A document and data assistant that lets your team upload files, ask questions, extract answers, and turn structured information into useful charts and summaries.

  • Upload contracts, reports, forms, SOPs, and spreadsheets
  • Ask natural-language questions around your documents
  • Generate quick visual charts from normal business data
  • Support secure environments, including air-gapped deployments
02

Workflow Automation

We automate work across inboxes, forms, ERP systems, CRMs, Slack, and internal tools so routine operational work keeps moving.

  • Watch a shared inbox and classify incoming requests
  • Draft or send replies based on rules and context
  • Create or update ERP and back-office records
  • Route approvals to humans only when needed
03

AgentX

An engineering-focused agent that works with your repository and creates draft pull requests based on instructions, requirement files, and connected workflows.

  • Understand repository context before drafting changes
  • Trigger from a dropped requirements file
  • Also trigger from email, Slack, or another system
  • Fit into an approval-friendly software delivery workflow
